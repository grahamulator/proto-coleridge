About Us

We are a diverse group of data scientists, economists, statisticians, software engineers, and more with a strong desire to make the world a better place. We believe there is an opportunity to substantially improve how data is used and analyzed, especially in the context of public policy.

What we value

  • Positive social impact: Our every project, action, and effort contributes positive social value either directly or indirectly by supporting some other project, action, or effort
  • Integrity & openness: We do not sell anything we would not ourselves buy; every project requires clear benefits and justification
  • Practical & robust progress: We do not sacrifice science for expedience, nor pragmatism for science; every effort is explicitly scoped and continually reassessed

Background

The Colerdige Initiative is the culmination of many years of work by the three principal Directors: Rayid Ghani, Frauke Kreuter, and Julia Lane. A key turning point was teaching the courses that then lead to their publishing, in collaboration with key co-authors, the book Big Data and Social Science.
